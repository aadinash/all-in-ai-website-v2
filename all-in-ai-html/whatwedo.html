WHAT WE DO

The goal of All In AI is to inspire a diverse community of students to consider a future in AI and empower them with the technical knowledge to do that. All In AI teaches at high schools and also provides an introductory online course on the learn tab. Peer-to-peer learning is part of the process, where students who have already taken the course can give back, teaching the course in the future.
