ABOUT US

Aadi Nashikkar

In 9th grade, I wanted to learn AI. Unfortunately, few courses were designed for beginners. The few courses intended for high schoolers were expensive and inaccessible. Still, I struggled, persevered, and learned as much as I could, never forgetting my path of difficulties. This is why I started All in AI. Research demonstrates that exposure to AI education prior to college exponentially raises the chance that students go on to pursue related careers. How can an AI revolution happen if many of those impacted are left behind? All In AI bridges this gap
